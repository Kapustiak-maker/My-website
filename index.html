How to Order

Follow these steps to place your order:

1. Join the Discord Server

Click the link below to join my Discord server where all communication and orders take place:

Join Discord Server

2. Contact Me

Once you're in the server, you can create a ticket in the dedicated channel or send me a direct message (DM). Both options are valid for starting your order.

Creating a Ticket: This allows us to have a private conversation thread specifically for your project, keeping everything organized.

Sending a DM: This is a quick way to get started. I'll then guide you through the process.

3. Provide Details

Let me know what you need. Please include as much detail as possible, such as:

  • The type of model or asset
  • Style reference (if you have one)
  • Intended use (e.g., for a Roblox game)
  • Your deadline

4. Get a Quote

I will review your request and provide an estimated price and timeline.

5. Make Payment

If you agree to the quote, we'll arrange payment. A 10% deposit is usually required to start.

6. Receive Your Work

Once the work is complete and payment is finalized, I will deliver the files to you via Discord.
